Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Is Music in Motion free to use?

Yes! We offer a free version with core features, and additional perks are available through our premium plans.

How do I reset my password?

Click on "Forgot Password?" on the login page and follow the instructions.

Concert & Event Planner

How do I find concerts near me?

Use the Concert Finder page to see shows based on your location and preferences.

Can I see what concerts my friends are going to?

Yes! Friends’ activity is shown on your event calendar when you’re connected.

Setlists & Playlists

How can I create a playlist?

Go to the Setlists page, click “Create Playlist,” and add your favorite songs.

Can I randomize my playlist?

Absolutely! Use the “Randomize” feature when editing your setlist.

Subscriptions

What are the subscription tiers?

We offer Free, Premium, and VIP plans. Each tier unlocks more features like ticket alerts, exclusive access, and genre-based communities.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Visit your account settings and select "Cancel Subscription."
